Visual Behaviours as Musical Structures

Generative geometry as musical structure

This research explores how spatial behaviours can generate musical structures in real time. A series of visual systems developed in TouchDesigner translate proximity, collision, density and synchronisation into MIDI events driving Ableton Live. Rather than composing fixed sequences, the visual logic itself becomes the instrument.
