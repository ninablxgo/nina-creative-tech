About

After four years in art and design school, I gradually shifted toward emerging technologies to expand the spatial and temporal dimensions of my work. My Master’s allowed me to experiment with real-time systems and interactive structures, while my professional experiences exposed me to the collaborative and technical frameworks required to produce immersive installations. I am now focused on designing environments that are not fixed forms, but evolving situations.

My practice moves between software, hardware and network-based systems, depending on the logic and scale of each installation. I am particularly interested in how individual actions can shape collective spatial experiences.

Nina Blagojevic — Creative Technologist
